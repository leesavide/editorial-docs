.. _workflow-action-count:

=================
Count
=================

*Counts words, sentences, lines, or characters in the input text*

The output is a number. You can use the result in a :ref:`workflow-action-math` action or as regular text.

* **Input:** The text to analyze
* **Output:** The resulting number, does not include any formatting (e.g. thousands separators)

Parameters
----------

* **What to Count:** Can be `Words`, `Sentences`, `Lines`, or `Characters`
