=================
Change Case
=================

*Changes the case of the input to UPPER CASE, lower case, or Title Case*

* **Input:** The text to convert
* **Output:** The converted text

Parameters
----------

* **Conversion Type:** The type of case conversion to perform. Note that the `Title Case` option simply converts the first letter of each word to upper case and does not make language-specific exceptions for prepositions or conjunctions.
