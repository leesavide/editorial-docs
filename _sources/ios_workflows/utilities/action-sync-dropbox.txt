=================
Sync with Dropbox
=================

*Starts syncing with Dropbox and waits until it's finished*

If no Dropbox account is linked, this action is simply ignored. Otherwise, a full sync is started, and the workflow continues after it's finished.

* **Input:** N/A
* **Output:** Same as input

Parameters
----------

This action has no configurable parameters.
